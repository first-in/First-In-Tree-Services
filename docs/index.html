SERVICES

Fully licensed and insured, we have you covered with all your residential or commercial needs for tree services

Fully licensed and insured, we have you covered with all your residential or commercial needs for tree services including trimming and pruning, storm damage clean up, live and dead tree removal, canopy raising, and standard tree maintenance and care.

Tree removal is an option if the tree is dead, or in decline, at high risk of failure, close enough to a structure to do significant damage if affected by a storm or heavy winds, or it just doesn't add to the landscape's visual appeal.
Pruning is essential for healthy trees, as well as a pleasing landscape. Large framework trees require periodic inspection and pruning to reduce the risk of large limb or total tree failure. Trees growing close to the house also require periodic pruning to prevent property damage.
Canopy raising when done properly can greatly improve the appeal of your property, reduce the amount of limbs falling in your yard, and let more light shine through on your yard as well.
In the unfortunate event that you have property damaged by a tree/trees during a storm we are able to respond promptly to your tree emergency.

HOW WE STARTED

Nick Waters started working in the tree industry for Asplundh Tree Expert Co. in the fall of 1999 where over the next few years he worked his way up to a Class A Foreman.

During his time at Asplundh they responded to the aftermath of many storms such as Hurricanes Isabell (in Richmond, VA) and Katrina (Miami, Florida). Working during these storms provided valuable on the job training and paved the way for the unknown future First In Tree Services.

In 2008 Nick got the opportunity to pursue every kids dream as a fire fighter for Richmond City! For 9 years he fought fires, met and helped countless people. He spent 5 of those years at the busiest Engine Company in Virginia where they responded to more than 4,000 emergency calls in a one year!

While working for the fire department Nick maintained his passion for tree work and worked part time for fellow fire fighter who ran a landscaping company. In 2014, with his 3rd child on the way, Nick and his wife Jacque decided to embrace his entrepreneurial spirit and started First In Tree Services. This allowed Nick to pursue his passion for tree work on his days off from the fire department.

Family is the highest priority in Nick's life and in 2017 after months of planning and discussion he and Jacque agreed that running the tree business full time would be best for their family. So as of June 2017 First In Tree Services became a full time business. Now Nick can fully focus on continuing excellence in tree work and customer service, and has more quality time to spend with Jacque and the kids.

Nick keeps high standards for his work and his employees. First In Tree Services employs several fire fighters to help out for part time work and all employees undergo a drug screen and backround check. We at First In Tree Services take extreme pride at providing a high level service and always have your properties best interest in mind. We do not take short cuts and we do take the extra steps to protect your property - we strive to leave your property just as good if not better than how we found it.

We look forward to being your number 1 tree service provider!
